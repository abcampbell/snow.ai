Long/Short Spread (Safe / Danger)

Rising = model working. The GFC (2007-2009) saw the spread collapse from 100 to 56 as "safe" banks were actually the most exposed to subprime. Post-GFC recovery took until 2012. Since then, steady gains.

Model Performance by Market Regime

The model was built for duration/rate risk (HTM exposure, capital ratios, vol signals). It excels at SVB-type crises but inverts during credit crises where off-balance-sheet exposure matters more than reported fundamentals.
