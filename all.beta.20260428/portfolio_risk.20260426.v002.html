Known accuracy gaps (do not over-interpret decimals):
  • Delta-normal VaR ignores gamma/vega. 1.48% daily 1σ doesn't capture vol shocks. A real ag or credit dislocation could move the book +/-10% NLV in a day that won't show up here — only in the named scenarios.
  • Vol-adj column uses 3y daily proxy vols (TLT for SONIA, CORN for corn etc.). SONIA actual vol is closer to 9.6% (front-end rates) than TLT's 14% — I've used the lower number where I knew it. Other themes use ETF-proxy vol.
  • Some sugar option strikes show δ=0. 4 of 22 sugar option positions had marketPrice=0 in the JSON snapshot, iv defaulted to 30%, BS gave 0.
  • 1-year covariance lookback. Captures recent regime, misses 2022-style shifts.
  • Live IV not pulled. IB's modelGreeks would be more accurate than BS-solved-from-premium. Requires live data subscription.
