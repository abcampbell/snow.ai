Known accuracy gaps (do not over-interpret):
  • Delta-normal VaR ignores gamma/vega. $1,658 daily 1σ does not capture vol shocks. A real ag or credit dislocation could move +/-10% NLV in a day that won't show up here — only in the named scenarios.
  • Some sugar option strikes show δ=0. 4 of 22 sugar option positions had marketPrice=0 in the JSON snapshot (illiquid Friday close), iv defaulted to 30%, BS gave 0. Real δ is small but non-zero.
  • 1-year covariance lookback. Captures recent regime, misses 2022-style. Conservative for current vol, optimistic for tail.
  • SONIA→TLT proxy in portfolio_risk_decomp overstates correlation between SONIA and IEI (both proxied via TLT). The actual rates curve correlation is more nuanced.
